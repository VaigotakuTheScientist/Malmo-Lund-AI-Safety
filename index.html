Malmö Lund AI Safety

A community in Skåne for discussing AI safety, alignment, governance, technical challenges, and the societal impacts of advanced AI.

Malmö AI Safety, Lund AI Safety, and Skåne AI Safety all live under the same roof here.

This site is intentionally simple: one page, static HTML, static CSS, no framework, no tracking circus.

About

Malmö Lund AI Safety is a local community for people who want a serious, grounded conversation about advanced AI: how it may affect society, science, labor, institutions, power, security, and human wellbeing, plus what can be done to steer development in safer and more beneficial directions.

The group is open to students, researchers, engineers, founders, policy-curious people, writers, and anyone in southern Sweden who wants something more thoughtful than either hype or doomposting.

Events

We host local meetups in Malmö, Lund, and nearby. The aim is simple: discuss AI safety, meet thoughtful people in the region, and build a serious community in Skåne.
